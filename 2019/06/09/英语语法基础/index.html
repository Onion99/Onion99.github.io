英语语法基础

Posted on 2019-06-09

語法

句子的结构

简单句

一个句子（一件事）

核心构成

世界是物质的，物质是运动的

物质+运动

n. + v. 主语+谓语

谓语分类

有实义

  • 不及物动词 vi. —>主谓
    • i swim.
    • Birds fly in the sky.
    • We walked on the street yesterday.
    • You look at me . (介宾结构)
  • 及物动词 vt.
    • 主谓宾
      • The bride kissed the groom.
      • I like English.
    • 主谓双宾
      • They offered me a vacant post.
      • They offered a vacant post to me.
      • i bought you a present .
      • i bought a present for you.
    • 主谓宾补
      • I find HongKong very beautiful.
      • I find HongKong a good place foe shoppong.
  • 区分 vt./vi.
    • 意思
    • 介词

无实义

  • 连系动词
    • 主系表
      • i very happy ？？？
      • i am very happy.
    • 系动词
      • 系动词没有实义，只起连接作用
      • be动词 （单独）
      • 表“变得”
        • get become turn go grow
      • 表“感觉”
        • look sound smell taste feel
      • 表“状态”
        • keep remain seem apper
谓语动词的变化-时态
态|时 过去 现在 将来 过去将来
一般 did do/does will do / be going to would do /being going to do
进行 was/were doing be doing will be doing
完成 had done have/has done will/shall have done
完成进行 had been doing have/has been doing will/shall been doing
一般过去时
  • 形式
    • V. 过去式
      • did
      • was(were)
  • 用法
    • 过去的动作，无关现在
    • He liked music
  • 变否定或疑问
  • 补充
    • V.过去式
      • 用于一般过去时
    • V.过去分词
      • 用于完成时
      • 被动语态
      • 不作谓语
一般现在时
  • 形式
    • V.原形/V. 第三人称单数（he,she,it,可数名词单数，不可数）
      • do/does
    • You like English
    • He likes English
  • 用法
    • 现在 经常性习惯性的动作
      • We have the Enflish class every day.
      • He often gets up late.
    • 现在的状态
      • i am a teacher
      • We are in China.
    • 永恒/表示客观事实
      • The earth is round.
      • The earth moves around the sun.
      • Knowledge is power.
      • Practice makes perfect.
  • 变否定或疑问
  • 补充
    • 与频率连用
      • every..
      • once a week, twice a month
      • three time as
      • alway
      • usually,often,frequently
      • sometime,occasionally
      • seldom,hardly
      • never
一般将来时
  • 形式
    • will
    • am/is/are going to +V.原形
  • 用法
    • 现在的将来
    • I will make a newplan tomorrow.
    • We are going to study abroad next year.
  • 变否定或疑问
过去将来时
  • 形式
    • would
    • was /were going to + V. 原形
  • 用法
    • 过去的将来
    • I said that I would become a cook in the future.
    • Tony finshed his work,and then he would leave for London.
现在完成时
  • 形式
    • have/has +done
  • 用法–>现在的之前
    • 现在全部完成
      • He has left.
    • 现在部分完成
      • We have studied English for ten years.
过去完成时
  • 形式
    • had done
  • 用法
    • 过去的之前
    • When he got there,she had left.
完成进行时

谓语动词的变化-语态-被动语态
适用范围
  • 及物动词
  • 加介词的不及物动词
形式
  • be (被动的时间-对应的时态及主语的单复数)+ done（被动的动作）
与情态动词相结合

谓语动词的变化-情态
用法
  • 表情态，推测
  • 情态动词+动词原形
  • 没有人称的变化
  • 时态变化有限
  • 情态动词+have done /be doing
    • have done 之前的事情
    • be doing 进行的事情
形式
现在时 过去时/委婉点
must 无 必须
can could 能够/可以
will would 愿意/将要
shall should 应该
may might 可以/可能
主语/宾语/表语的变化：
  • doing/todo
    • 可以作主语
      • Laughing probably has great influence on health.
      • it no use to talk about dreams without trying.
    • 作宾语
      • We enjoy reading books in the library.
      • The member of the board decide to vote against the new plan.
  • 多个并列
    • Science and technology will develop the provess
    • That doesn’t mean sitting down and doing nothing at all.
简单句的补充
限定词/形容词副词/介词短语

介词短语-介词 prep

  • in on at
  • from to
  • into onto
  • with without
  • of
    • a of b = b 的 a
  • by
  • for
  • about
  • before after
  • since until
  • during
  • between among
  • across through
  • against
  • like as

非谓语动词

  • doing-主动
    • Passing planes can be heard night and day.
    • The teacher came into classroom,holding a book in his hand
  • done-被动
    • He bought a used car
    • The teacher came into the classroom,followed by five students.
  • to do-目的
    • i am coming to see you.
    • i have a lot of homework to do;

同位语/插入语-补充成分

My teacher , Mr.Lee, is coming to the meeting.

My teacher,together with his colleagues, is coming to the meeting.

并列句/复合句

多个句子连接（多件事）

并列句-表多件事一样重要
  • 顺接
    • …and….
    • both….and….
    • not only …… but …….as well
    • not only ,,,,,,, but also
  • 转折
    • …but…
    • ….yet…
    • ….while…
  • 选择
    • …or…
    • …..either…or..或者或者
    • neither…..not…既不也不
  • 因果
    • …for…
    • …so…
复合句-表多件事重要性不一样（主句+从句）
名词性从句

四类从句写法相同，位置不同

主语从句

  • 不能省略that
  • 表达是否，只能用whether
  • 从句可放句首，也可以句尾

宾语从句（动宾，介宾，形容词+宾）

  • 一个句子作宾语，放到另一个句子中
  • 陈述句变宾语：前加that，I know (that) you are right
  • 特殊疑问变宾语：调整语序，主语提前,What will she say->I know what she will say
  • 一般疑问变宾语：调整语序，前加是否,Is he happy? ->I know whether/ if he is happy

表语从句

  • 同宾语从句
  • 不能省略that
  • 最常位语Be动词或系动词后

同位语从句

  • 解释说明n.
  • 位置：需要解释的抽象n.后（fact,idea,）
定语从句
  • 含义
    • 一个句子作定语去修饰限定名词
  • 写法
    • This is the job.
    • I have dreamed of the job for years.
    • This the job which I have dreamed of for years.
      • job -先行词
      • which-关系词
      • 从句
    • 先行词与关系词
      • 物 which/that
      • 人 who/whom/that
      • 人的/物的 whose
      • 时间 when
      • 地点 where
      • 原因 why
  • 分类
    • 限定性
    • 非限定性
      • 前面名词范围明确
      • 逗号隔开
      • 可以省略
      • 关系词不能省略
  • 注意
    • 定语从句每个关系词都有成分意义
    • 关系词作宾语可以省略
    • 从句在第二谓语动词之前结束
